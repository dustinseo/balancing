Tolerance

Unbalance in the bearing planes versus correction planes

  1. Unbalance vectors can be calculated precisely for other planes(on a rigid rotor)
  2. Unbalance tolerances are no vectors, but areas.
  3. Unbalance Tolerances are the maximum Amount of unbalance permissible.
  4. The angular position is normally not considered.
  5. Unbalance tolerance cannot be calculated precisely for other planes.
  6. The worst case is always considered.
  7. Therefore either the static unbalance or couple unbalance is usually corrected better than necessary
