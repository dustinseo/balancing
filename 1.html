Test Of Calibration

Determine test results of the calibration accuracy

Result determination and instruction:
1)The First step is to test the initial unbalance of the calibration sample part under detection.
2)The second step is to test the unbalance value after automatic system compensation.
* The theoretical value should be less than the initial unbalance of the rotor.
* Compensation aims to remove the influence of the initial unbalance of the calibration sample part on the calibration benchmark measurement.
* When the initial unbalance of the rotor is very small, this step is ignored.
3)The third step is to test the measurement of the weight on the plane 1 of the calibration sample part.
* Measurement result of unbalance on the plane 1 : the value error should be less than +/-5% of the theorical value.
The angle error should be less than theoretical angle 1 degree.
* The separation ratio of the measurement of the plane 1 to it of the plane 2 should be more than 30:1.
